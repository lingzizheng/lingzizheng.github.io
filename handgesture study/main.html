Hands

In this project, I ask people who know me(regardless of how closed we are) to interact with my hands. Among those people, there are people that I just meet for a few times, faculties and closed friends. I am interested in seeing their way of interacting with my hands, often people closer to me will have uncommon way to touch my hands.
